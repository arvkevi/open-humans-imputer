Description
The goal of Imputer is to provide users with a more comprehensive picture of their genome. Direct-to-consumer genetics companies, like 23andMe, only genotype a small fraction of the genome. Researchers are finding new genetic locations, not covered by direct-to-consumer genetic tests, that associated with traits and diseases at a rapid pace. Imputer leverages the 1000 Genomes Project reference data to provide users with genotype estimates at additional locations in their genome.
Data Access
Requests access to your genetic data in Open Humans (e.g. 23andMe)
